An opera with Foley, from the radio play by Samuel Beckett. Performed by live singers, a “breath control” orchestra — each keyboard and breath controller with its own speaker, and oversized wooden Foley machines.

Live at The Box, Los Angeles. April 21 & 28, 2018.

Singers: Laurel Irene, Gabriel Manro, Jodie Landau, Lauren Davis, Matthew Soibelman, Sean Gabel, Allen Pearcy Galeana
Players: Thomas Kotcheff, Basia Bochenek, Christoffer Schunk, Ben Phelps, Matthew Blackmar, Richard An, Katherine Redlus, Matthew Cook, Dustin Donahue
Production: Adrian Garcia (assistant producer, tech director), Jack Cheng (lighting designer), David Donaldson (title operator), Monique Prieto (costumes)
